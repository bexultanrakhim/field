About me

My name is Bexultan and I am robotics engineer. I work at company that develops self driving cars and autonomous robots. Before that, I did research in robotics and machine learning. My work is related to the topics like control theory, reinforcement learning, computer vision. My interests are: natural sciences, philosophy of mind, cognitive sciences and linguistics. At my free time, I develop small scale AI bots.

I like to write about my observations and share my thoughts with my friends. Sometimes I can spend all night reading research papers and in the morning start spamming my friends about some extremely controversial ideas (yes, I am aware they are controversial). I think of it as a stimulus to brain to think critically.

This is how I came up with idea to create a blog.

My experience with blogs

I've tried to create blogs before. But I did not even imagine how hard it is to write nice and comprehensive text. Many times I've done same mistakes of over-complicating things or over-simplifying. Previously I have tried to write about philosophy, attempt that makes me feel ashamed. Needless to say, it was overconfidence in my knowledge and abilities. For this reason, I will abstain from writing on topcis I am not familiar with.

I want to create very nice blog of my thoughts. To save my knowledge in a way, that is manageable and easily accessible to everyone. There are so many things I want to document. 

My mind is filled with a lot of information. But recently, I started to forget. Time is really merciless. These might be my futile efforts to save something about me.

You can thing of this blog as my cognitive offloading. I am uploading the here all my knowledge. I hope some of you find it useful in a way. 

What is the plan?

This is a blog, so I am writing anythings that is of my interest. I will be modifying older posts by editing possible errors and managing new content in a form of a blog. Send me message if you see any errors! Each blog will be assigned 4 digit number. Sort of a way to distinguish them in a temporal order.

Let's begin this long road together...
