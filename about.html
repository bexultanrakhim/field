As always, I want to write about many things.

I've tried to create blogs before. But I did not even imagine how hard it is to write nice and comprehensive text. Many times I've done same mistakes of over-complicating things or over-simplifying. I even feel ashamed of the fact that I have tried to write about philosophy. I was too confident in my abilities and now, finally, I gained little bit of humbleness. I won't write about things I am not familiar with anymore. I will be writing only about things of my interest.

I want to create very nice blog of my thoughts. Save some of my knowledge in a way, that is manageable and easily accessible to everyone. There are so many things I want to document. 

My mind is filled with a lot of information. But recently, I started to forget. Time is really merciless. These might be my futile efforts to save something about me.

You can thing of this blog as my cognitive offloading. I am uploading the here all my knowledge. I hope some of you find it useful in a way. 

What is the plan?

This is a blog, so I will be writing anythings that is of my interest. I will be modifying older posts by editing possible errors and managing new content in a form of a blog. Send me message if you see any errors!. Each blog will be assigned 4 digit number. Sort of a way to distinguish them in a temporal order.

Let's begin this long road together...
